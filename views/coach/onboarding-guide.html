Getting Started

Welcome to ClosureAI!

Follow this guide to set up your AI coaching app and start helping your clients gain clarity. You'll be up and running in just a few minutes.

1 Customize Your Branding

Make your app feel like your own by adding your logo, colors, and business information. Your clients will see your branding throughout their experience.

  • Logo: Upload your business logo (recommended size: 200x200px)
  • Colors: Set your primary brand color to match your website
  • Business Name: This appears in the app header and emails
Customize Branding

2 Configure Your Settings

Set up your coaching preferences and customize how the AI interacts with your clients.

  • Coaching Style: Define the tone and approach the AI should use
  • Target Audience: Describe who your ideal clients are
  • Custom Prompts: Add specific instructions or context for the AI
Open Settings

3 Add Your First Client

You can add clients manually from your dashboard or share your app link and let them sign up themselves.

  • Manual: Add clients by email from your dashboard
  • Self-signup: Share your app URL and clients can create their own accounts

4 Share Your App

Once you're set up, share your unique app URL with your clients. They can access your AI coaching sessions anytime, anywhere.

How AI Sessions Work

When your clients start a session, the AI guides them through a structured conversation to help them gain clarity on their challenges. Here's what happens:

  1. Client logs into your app and starts a new session
  2. AI asks thoughtful questions to understand their situation
  3. Through guided conversation, clients gain new perspectives
  4. Sessions are saved and can be reviewed anytime
  5. You can view all client sessions from your dashboard

Note: The AI is trained to ask clarifying questions and help clients think through problems - it won't give direct advice unless that's part of your coaching style settings.
