What’s a micro-app, and why should you care?

Most businesses either send people to a generic chatbot or hand them another PDF they’ll never read. A micro-app does something different: it walks your prospect or client through a short, guided experience that ends with a clear result.

Instead of dumping information on them, it helps them answer one important question, make one decision, or see one snapshot of their situation — in just a few minutes, without you needing to be there.

That tiny win builds trust. It shows how you think. And it moves them closer to booking a call, scheduling a visit, or saying “yes” to the next step.

  • Runs 24/7 without you babysitting it.
  • Delivers a small but meaningful win your people actually feel.
  • Positions you as the helpful expert instead of another loud ad.
  • Replaces boring lead magnets and static downloads with something interactive.

What could a micro-app do for your business?

Here are a few ways different businesses could use an AI micro-app. The engine is the same — we just change the problem, the questions, and the language. This is not a comprehensive list, it's just to get your wheels turning.

Think about the top 2 or 3 question you get repeatedly from prospects or clients. Let's build a micro-app to answer those questions for you (or more importantly, for them).

HVAC – “Emergency Heat Triage”

Homeowners answer a few questions and get clear next steps: try a simple fix, shut things down, or call you now. You look like the hero either way.

Real Estate – “Buy Now or Wait?”

Buyers walk through their situation and get a simple, human explanation of what makes sense next, plus an easy way to book a call with you.

Financial Advisors – “Retirement Readiness Snapshot”

Visitors answer a short series of questions and receive a readiness summary and suggested talking points for your first meeting.

Insurance – “Coverage Gap Checkup”

Prospects get a quick sense of where they might be underprotected, along with a gentle nudge to review those areas with you.

Home Services – “DIY or Call a Pro?”

A short decision helper that tells people when it’s safe to handle something themselves and when they should book you instead.

Coaches & Mentors – “Clarity Session Tool”

A branded, between-sessions space like ClosureAI that uses your language and frameworks so clients stay grounded in your process.
